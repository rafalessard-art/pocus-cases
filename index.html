About this series

These cases are designed for small-group or self-directed resident teaching. Answer keys are collapsible in the HTML versions, so learners can attempt each question before revealing the model answer, and every case can be printed to PDF from the browser.

The perioperative evidence base — particularly for GLP-1 receptor agonists — is consensus-driven and changing quickly. Verify against your institution's current policy and the latest society guidance before applying any of this to patient care.
